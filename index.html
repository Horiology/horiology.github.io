About Kanna

Kanna is a dragon that belongs to the destructive faction. She follows Tohru, whom she finds as a sisterly figure, to the human world to bring her back, but then assumes that Kobayashi had seduced Tohru to make her stay. So she went to confront Kobayashi only to stay with her in the end. Kanna then becomes attached to Kobayashi, viewing Kobayashi as a motherly figure.

"That's wicked"- Kanna Kamui
