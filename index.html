Tinolang Manok

  • 1 KG Chicken
  • Malunggay leaves
  • Hot pepper leaves
  • Patis
  • Knorr chicken-cubes
  • Garlic
  • Oil of your choice (minus crude oil)
  • Ginger
  • 1.5 L of water
  • Papaya
  • Pepper

This is how you make a Filipino Favorite dish, Tinolang Manok.

Step 1

Heat the oil you have chosen in a pot.

Step 2

Saute garlic, onion and ginger then add the black pepper.

Step 3

When the onion starts to get soft, add the chicken. Cook for 15m.

Step 4

Pour the water and let it boil. Cover and set the heat to low. Boil it for 40 minutes.

Step 5

Add the Knorr chicken cube and sayote or papaya, then stir. Cover and cook for 5 minutes. Finally, Put the malunggay in the pot and pour the fish sauce. Continue to cook for 2 more minutes and serve!
